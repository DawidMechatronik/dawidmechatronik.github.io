CP2 with external PCP tank

To make this modification you need 4 parts: PCP adapter, hose, valve and PCP bottle.

1. PCP adapter

You need adapter which makes possible to connect HP hose with 8mm quick connect to air rifle.

  • You can buy one in my shop. You screw it instead of CO2 12g cartridge and CO2 cartridge push screw. Link to Etsy

2. HP hose with 8mm quick connect fitting

You need hose to connect air rifle adapter with CO2 tank. At first I bought less flexible hose with straight ends ... it works, but it was not nice to use.

  • Less flexible hose with straight ends (item: "Straight 52inch") Link to Aliexpress

I decided to buy second hose, more flexible with angled end. This is much better choice then previous one. Ignore "T" connector and bleed valve for a moment - it is nice to have, but it is optional.

  • More flexible hose with one angled end (item: "40 Inch-100CM") Link to Aliexpress

3. Bleeding valve for PCP hose (optional)

It is nice to equip PCP hose with bleeding valve. You can safely discharge hose and disconnect it. Without bleeding valve you need to dry fire air rifle, to drop pressure inside the hose.

  • Tee connector body with 3x 1/8" NPT threads (item: "NPT Thread","1/8"") Link to Aliexpress

  • Bleed valve with 1/8" thread Link to Aliexpress

  • Male quick connector with internal 1/8" thread (item: "Fill Nipple(F1-8NPT)") Link to Aliexpress

4. Compressed air power source

At the end you need source of compressed air. So you need HP bottle, pressure regulator and adapter with quick connector.

4.1. PCP tank

I found this one, looks like nice quality bottle. Working pressure is 300 bar (4350 psi). I pump it to 4000 psi max with my hand pump. With 0,25 liter bottle charged to 4000 psi you can make 100-120 shoots with power around 14 J.

  • I tank with capacity 0,25 liter and 0,5 liter (item: "0.25L Round Bottle" or "0.5L Round Bottle"). Link to Aliexpress

4.2. Pressure regulator

This one is more expensive than regulator with constant pressure but gives you ability to adjust the output pressure using hex key. You can also set "0 psi" pressure which is equal to closed valve - then you can disconnect hose from tank without need of emptying the tank. You can also buy more accurate pressure gauge, for example 0 to 1500 or 2000 psi. Remember to add some oil to adjuster screw housing, it works much smother and easier.

  • 0-3000 psi pressure regulator with M18x1,5 thread (item: "M18"). Link to Aliexpress

  • Pressure gauge. You one with M10x1 thread. Recommended max pressure 1500 psi, 2000 psi, 3000 psi, 200 bar
    (item: "1500psi- M10" or "2000psi- M10" or "3000psi- M10" or "200bar-M10"). Link to Aliexpress

4.3. Adapter

At the end you need quick connect adapter installed at output thread of the pressure regulator.

  • Adapter with female 8mm quick connector. (item: "8mm Female QD"). Link to Aliexpress
